# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Indonesian and Singaporean CEOs, founders, and C-suite executives (typically 35–55) building companies in high-growth sectors. Busy decision-makers who arrive on desktop, likely after a referral or seeing MAXY AI content. They have attended AI workshops before and left underwhelmed. Skeptical of hype — respond to practitioner credibility and concrete outcomes.

## Product Purpose

AI CEO Circle landing page for MAXY AI's exclusive executive mentoring program. Exists to convince elite business leaders this program is categorically different from the AI workshops they've tried — demonstrating practitioner depth, extreme exclusivity (10 seats), and concrete deliverables (one AI agent built, Digital CEO Twin activated). Primary conversion: application submitted.

## Brand Personality

Authoritative, practitioner-led, exclusive. Not "AI hype." Not a course. A circle.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI course platforms (Coursera, LinkedIn Learning) — too accessible, no scarcity
- Typical SaaS blue-and-white landing pages — corporate, no soul, no edge
- Over-designed agency sites with too many animations — distraction over substance
- Any copy that says "join the AI revolution" — pure hype, zero credibility

## Design Principles

1. **Show, don't explain.** The site itself should feel built by people who actually understand AI and executive communication — not by a template.
2. **Exclusivity is the product.** Every design decision reinforces scarcity and curation (10 seats, application required, practitioner-led).
3. **Practitioner confidence.** Bold, unhurried typography and layout. No icons for everything. No bullet lists where prose works better.
4. **Dark, warm, editorial.** Magma/navy aesthetic communicates seriousness without corporate coldness. Lava orange as the one accent — heat, not hype.
5. **Indonesian-Singaporean executive register.** Not Silicon Valley. Not Jakarta startup. Senior, regional, bilingual.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA target. Bilingual (English/Bahasa Indonesia) — headings must wrap cleanly in both languages. `prefers-reduced-motion` respected for all animations.
